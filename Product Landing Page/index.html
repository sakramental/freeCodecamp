About BIOKEDR®

Biokedr® is a research and production company engaged in the development and production of natural food products and natural cosmetics.

Biokedr® is the official sponsor of the Kyokushin Kaikan Federation of Russia.

We were chosen as an additional aid for the recovery of athletes during intensive training and recovery, as the cedar cone purified from resin contains more than 130 micro and macroelements that favorably contribute to the body of any person, at any age.

Our Product

Biokedr® is a 100% natural product consisting of Far Eastern cedar cones, uniquely purified from resin. Full of vitamins, micro and macro elements, phytoncides and tannins and created for a comprehensive recovery of the whole body.

Patent products

The raw material "Crushed pine cone of the Far Eastern cedar, purified from resin" has passed quality certification in Europe, Russia, and Asia.

All necessary approvals have been obtained for the sale of Biokedr products in European countries, which confirms the high quality and safety of the product.
