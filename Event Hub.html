All-Russian Summer School FKR 2025

On June 19-22, 2025, the Chekhov Sports and Recreation Center (Moscow Region) will host All-Russian Summer School of the Federation Kyokushin Kaikan of Russia 2025. 

This year, one of the invited guests will be Maxim Yekimov, the brightest fighter of our national team, a native of the EAO and a prominent representative of the Far Eastern Kyokushin school, winner of the Absolute World Championship, Champion of Russia, winner of the Japanese Championships, Senpai, Master of Sports of International class, 2nd Dan.

Past Events

The Mitsuden Club team has two 2025 World Champions

On April 25-27, Tokyo hosted the main event in the world of Kyokushin, the 7th Men's and Women's World Championships. 
Rashad Huseynov and Alexandra Kazaryan, representing the Moscow Mitsuden Club, became World Champions at this important and prestigious tournament. Rashad took 1st place in the men's weight category up to 90 kg, Alexandra - 1st place in the women's weight category up to 55 kg.
Congratulations to the athletes and their mentors with such a great result and wish them further successful performances!

Intensive course of IKO Honbu

The intensive course of the IKO Honbu was completed, which was attended by a delegation of the FKR led by Viktor Borisovich Shihan Belov. This year, the course was held in one of the most historically significant places in Japan - Nikko. Training sessions under the guidance of leading Kyokushin Kaikan mentors took place in the mecca of kendo - dojo Butokuden (the place where kendo was revived), the Takugawa Temple Complex.
At the end of the course, a dan test was held at Daikanyama Honbu Dojo.
