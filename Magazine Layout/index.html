Sosai Masutatsu Oyama's birthday

July 27 is the Special Day

On this day, a great master was born. Masutatsu Oyama, the man who created Kyokushin and thus united millions of people around the world. His philosophy continues to live on in the hearts and minds of every Kyokushin. On this day, memorable training sessions are held in different countries and cities in honor of Sosai Masutatsu Oyama's birthday. We thank everyone who attended the training and paid tribute to the memory of the great Master. Your presence is a sign that his work lives on and continues to inspire new generations.

Masutatsu Ōyama

(大山 倍達, Ōyama Masutatsu; 27 July 1923[4] – 26 April 1994)

more commonly known as Mas Oyama, was a Zainichi Korean karate master who founded Kyokushin Karate, considered the first and most influential style of full contact karate.

I have devoted my whole life to karate. I was alone, but now, all over the world, 10 million young followers are following me this way. If there is a first one, then there will definitely be a second one, a third one, and tens of thousands after the third one.

Masutatsu Oyama
